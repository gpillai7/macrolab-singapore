Geo-Economic Watch

Short notes on current developments that could move the next release.

  • Supply chain disruptions and route changes affecting trade costs.
  • Technology restrictions shaping electronics and semiconductor exposure.
  • Energy price volatility passing into freight, transport, and imported inflation.
  • China demand and global manufacturing softness affecting outward-facing sectors.

Why this page structure works

  • Each section is broken into a short commentary card plus a small set of simple metric cards.
  • The layout stacks naturally on smaller screens, so the page works as a mobile-responsive observatory.
  • Short annotations make the page usable for both experts and non-specialists.
  • Forecast or nowcast cards can be added later without redesigning the page.
