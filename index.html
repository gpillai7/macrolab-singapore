Briefing

What MacroLab is building

MacroLab is not a generic macro dashboard. It is a sensemaking platform that translates global developments into meaningful signals for Singapore’s economy, sectors, and business environment.

Observatory

Global shock detection

We monitor trade, policy, capital, energy, and supply chain developments that shape Singapore’s external environment.

Translation

Singapore-focused interpretation

We connect external events to local transmission channels such as exports, inflation, manufacturing, logistics, finance, and domestic demand.

Product

Decision-ready outputs

We package indicators and analysis into concise monthly snapshots, sector notes, and geo-economic briefings.
